BACKGROUND

Before building sites, I spent time in customer service — which shapes how I work now: quick to respond, comfortable handling questions and requests directly, and used to being the person people can actually reach. That, plus hands-on social media management, is part of what "long-term partner" means here — not just code, but the ongoing communication a real site and brand need.

HOW I BUILD

I use AI tools as part of my workflow — the same way developers use frameworks, compilers, or autocomplete. Every decision on structure, data, and user experience is mine, and I can walk through and explain any part of the code I ship. AI helps me move faster; it doesn't replace understanding what I'm building.
